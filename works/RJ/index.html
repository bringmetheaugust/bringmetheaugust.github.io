Probably everyone has ever tried to make Romeo and Juliet in a modern way, trying to surpass Shakespeare, trying to surpass the impossible. Perhaps we, too, each time embody the roles of our actors with the same dream. But time does not stand still and with every, even the most insignificant era, we have more and more 'soil' and opportunities to convey as much of our modern view of love, sorrow and the theater as a whole.
Our modest theater will be very grateful to each viewer, every flowers thrown on the stage, every tear for sincere love.
What is love? Madness from frenzy, Playing fire, leading to a fire..
